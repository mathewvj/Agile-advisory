TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) Filing

Accurate and timely Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) filing services to ensure compliance with income tax regulations.

Ensure timely and accurate TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) filing to stay compliant with tax regulations and avoid penalties. Businesses and individuals making payments such as salaries, rent, professional fees, and contractor payments must deduct TDS and file quarterly returns with the Income Tax Department.

We assist in:

  • TDS computation & deduction
  • Preparation & filing of quarterly TDS returns (Form 24Q, 26Q, 27Q, etc.)
  • TDS corrections & challan payments
  • Generating and issuing Form 16 & Form 16A

Let us handle your TDS compliance while you focus on growing your business.

File your TDS returns on time—hassle-free and error-free!

ROC Compliance & Annual Filings

Comprehensive Registrar of Companies (ROC) compliance services including annual returns and regulatory document filings.

Stay compliant with Registrar of Companies (ROC) regulations by filing your annual returns on time. All LLPs and Private Limited Companies in India must submit annual filings with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) to maintain legal compliance and avoid penalties.

Our services include:

  • Event-based filings (change in directors, shareholding, etc.)
  • Drafting of board resolutions & minutes

We ensure error-free and timely submissions, keeping your business compliant with government regulations.
